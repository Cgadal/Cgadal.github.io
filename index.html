Hi!
I am a Postdoctral researcher at the Institut de Mécanique des Fluides de Toulouse, driving experiments on turbidity currents.

Before, I was doing my PhD on sand dunes at the Institut de physique du globe de Paris. My main research interests are:
  • instabilities, patterns resulting from different couplings (fluid/grains, fluid/chemical reactions)
  • granular flows, sediment transport, suspensions (dunes, turbidity currents)
  • quantitative analysis of landscape morphodynamics
  • open research: distribution of analysis codes/research data (F. A. I. R.)
